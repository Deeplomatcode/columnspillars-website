Why CivicGuardian exists

The information is there. It just isn't connected.

Working directly with vulnerable adults through Citizens Advice, the pattern was clear — social care failures were rarely caused by absence of information. They were caused by fragmentation. The right signal existed in one system. The risk indicator sat in another. The practitioner never saw both at once.

CivicGuardian was designed to close that gap — using AI to synthesise the fragmented data that already exists in your systems, and surface the full picture to the right practitioner before it becomes a crisis.

Capabilities

Built for the complexity of real social care

Case Synthesis

Aggregates and summarises fragmented case information across systems, creating a coherent single view of each service user's situation.

Risk Stratification

Dynamically scores and ranks risk across your entire caseload — updated continuously, not at review intervals.

Pattern Recognition

Identifies early warning indicators: missed contacts, housing deterioration, behavioural changes, multi-agency signals.

Multi-Agency Coordination

Flags when multi-agency involvement is warranted. Supports information sharing within GDPR compliance.

Audit Trail

Every recommendation logged with full evidence and reasoning. Supports serious case reviews and demonstrates due diligence.

Outcome Tracking

Measures intervention effectiveness over time, building evidence for commissioning decisions.
