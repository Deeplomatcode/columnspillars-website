CivicGuardian AI

An AI platform that synthesises fragmented social care data, identifies escalating risk patterns, and surfaces prioritised alerts to practitioners — before vulnerable adults reach crisis point.

Born from direct experience volunteering at Citizens Advice UK, where fragmented data systems were failing the people who needed help most. CivicGuardian is the architectural response to that problem.
