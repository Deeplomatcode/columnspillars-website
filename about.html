About

The work speaks for itself. So does the thinking behind it.

Columns & Pillars is an architecture-led technology firm. We build AI and cloud systems that connect technology to business outcomes — measurably, accountably, and without the overhead of a Big Four engagement.

Why Columns & Pillars exists

Most organisations don't fail at technology because they lack tools. They fail because their technology decisions aren't connected to their business goals — and by the time that becomes visible, the cost is already significant. Columns & Pillars Tech was founded to close that gap.

We combine cloud architecture expertise, AI integration capability, and real business acumen to deliver technology that actually works at scale — built on AWS, designed for your context, and owned through delivery.

Incorporated in England & Wales in August 2025. Based in Oxford, UK. Our first product, CivicGuardian AI, reached the Top 50 as a Finalist in the AWS Global 10,000 AIdeas Competition, Social Impact Category. NYM Services Limited, an Oxford-based health and social care provider, has agreed to participate in an initial pilot evaluation.

What we stand for

Directness

You'll always know where things stand, even when that's not what you were hoping to hear.

We don't hand off and disappear. If something we designed isn't working, we stay engaged until it is.

The cost of no architecture oversight
