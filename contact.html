FAQ

Common questions

Is the discovery call really free?

Yes — genuinely. No invoice, no expectation of commitment. The purpose is to understand your situation and see whether there's a genuine fit. If there isn't, we'll say so.

What if I don't know exactly what I need?

"I'm not sure what we need" is one of the most common starting points. Come with a problem or a concern, not a fully-formed brief. That's what the discovery call is for.

Do you work with early-stage companies?

Occasionally, if the fit is right. We work with organisations at various stages — from scale-ups navigating rapid growth to established businesses managing complex transformation. If you have a genuine architecture challenge, it's worth a conversation.

How quickly can you start?

Typically 2–4 weeks from agreement. We don't overcommit and we don't take on work we can't deliver to the standard we hold ourselves to.
