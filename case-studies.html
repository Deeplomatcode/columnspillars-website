Featured Build

CivicGuardian AI — from Citizens Advice volunteer to AWS Global Semifinalist

A fully architected AI safeguarding platform, built on Amazon Bedrock Nova, entered into the AWS Global 10,000 AIdeas Competition — and recognised as a Semifinalist in the Social Impact category.

The Problem

Volunteering directly with vulnerable adults through Citizens Advice UK, the same pattern appeared in every complex case: the information needed to prevent a crisis already existed — in a GP record, a housing note, a missed welfare call log. It just wasn't connected.

Social workers were managing caseloads of 28+ service users with no system capable of synthesising signals across fragmented data sources. Risk escalated invisibly until it became a formal safeguarding event — or worse.

The Architecture Decision

Rather than build another case management system, the architecture decision was to build an intelligence layer — something that sits above existing systems, reads from them via API, and surfaces synthesised risk signals to practitioners without requiring any change to their existing workflow.

Amazon Bedrock Nova was selected for its enterprise data isolation guarantees, UK data residency in eu-west-2, and its ability to process unstructured case notes at scale without model training on sensitive data.

Problem Patterns

The types of problems we're built to solve

These patterns are drawn from real situations we've encountered and studied. They illustrate the approach we take — not claimed client outcomes.

Architecture Audit

"We know something's wrong with our cloud spend — we just don't know what or where."

The pattern

Organisations that migrated to cloud 2–4 years ago without architecture oversight typically have significant waste embedded in their infrastructure — idle compute, over-provisioned storage, unreviewed vendor contracts, and data flows that no longer match their compliance posture.

Our approach

A structured 4-week Architecture Audit: current-state mapping, vendor contract review, data flow analysis, security posture assessment, and a prioritised remediation roadmap — delivered as both a technical report and a board-ready executive summary.

What typically surfaces

  • Significant infrastructure waste from over-provisioning
  • Unreviewed auto-renewing contracts
  • Data exposure risks not visible to leadership
  • A clear, prioritised path to remediation
Fractional SA Retainer

"Our technology project is in trouble and we have no independent technical voice."

The pattern

A systems integrator or vendor is delivering something that doesn't match the agreed specification. The internal team lacks the technical authority to challenge it. The board is receiving reassurance rather than reality. Budget is eroding and the delivery timeline keeps moving.

Our approach

Join as Fractional SA mid-project. Review what's been built against what was agreed. Provide an independent technical report to the board. Chair architecture reviews, challenge the integrator with evidence, and stay engaged through to successful delivery.

What this changes

  • Board gets honest technical picture, not vendor narrative
  • Delivery scope renegotiated with evidence
  • Project recovered without external disclosure
  • Architecture documented for future maintainability
AI Transformation

"We want to use AI but we don't know where to start or what's actually safe to build."

The pattern

Leadership has identified AI as a strategic priority. The engineering team has experimented with tools. But there's no coherent architecture, no data readiness assessment, no governance framework, and no clear line between what's genuinely valuable and what's hype.

Our approach

AI readiness assessment first — data architecture, compliance posture, integration patterns. Then a prioritised use case roadmap: what to build, in what order, on what foundation. Delivery of the first use case end-to-end, with Amazon Bedrock where appropriate.

What this produces

  • Clear AI strategy with business case for each use case
  • Data architecture ready for AI workloads
  • First production AI integration delivered
  • Governance framework your board can stand behind
